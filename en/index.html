Fart Facts

The average person farts 14 times a day

Volume ranges from 0.5 to 2 liters. Most is odorless: nitrogen, oxygen, CO2, methane.

Classification of Farts

  • The Silent Killer – silent but deadly.
  • The Loud One – noisy but hardly smells.
  • The Squeaker – tiny, sudden bubble.
  • The Machine Gun – rapid-fire series.
  • The Lazy One – slow and warm.

Farts & Health

Holding in gas is harmful – it can cause bloating and pain. Regular release is key to comfort. If farts are too frequent or smelly, check your diet and microbiome.

Tip: add fiber to your diet gradually to avoid excessive gas.
